Ana Hickmann Finance Overview

Modeling Career and Financial Impact

Ana Hickmann’s modeling career has been nothing short of spectacular. She has walked the runway for some of the biggest names in fashion, such as Giorgio Armani, Roberto Cavalli, and Victoria’s Secret.

These high-profile gigs not only catapulted her into international fame but also brought in substantial earnings. Modeling contracts and endorsements with brands like L’Oréal and Nivea have significantly contributed to her wealth.

Business Ventures and Brand AH

Ana Hickmann’s entrepreneurial spirit shines through her brand, AH, which was launched in 2002. This brand is a powerhouse, generating around R$400 million annually.

The AH brand encompasses a wide range of products, including clothing, perfumes, bags, jewelry, watches, shoes, eyeglasses, sunglasses, and cosmetics. The success of her nail polish line, which sold over 6 million units in Brazil in 2014, is a testament to her business acumen.

Additionally, her eyewear line is sold in over 55 countries, making it a significant contributor to her income.

Television Hosting and Earnings

Hickmann’s transition from modeling to television was smooth and lucrative. She joined the Rede Record network in 2004, presenting a fashion segment on Tudo a Ver.

From 2005 to 2009, she co-hosted the daytime show Hoje em Dia, and later hosted her own show, Tudo É Possível. These roles have not only increased her visibility but also her earnings.
