Daisy Head’s Profession

Daisy made her acting debut in the television series “Feather Boy” in the lead role of Kate Barber in 2004. The next year, she co-starred in an episode of “Rose and Maloney” alongside her father in the role of Daniella Terry. In the year 2010, she made her acting debut as Chloe Chambers in the film “The Last Seven,” with Danny Dyer. In the same year, she co-starred alongside Jake Roche in the CBBC television musical film “Rules of Love.”

In 2012, she was cast in the series “The Proxy” as Sarah, a recurring role. Her major breakout role came in 2015, when she played Grace in Freeform’s murder-drama series “Guilt.” Daisy’s performance was favorably received despite the fact that the series only lasted ten episodes. Her career took off after she starred in the 2016 fantasy romance film “Fallen,” which was based on Arriane Alter’s book of the same name. In the same year, she was cast as Alexia in the underworld film “Underworld: Blood Wars.”
