Beyonc pregnant with twins: See all the amazing pictures from her photoshoot

May 2024 · 2 minute read

    If you thought Beyoncé's pregnancy announcement was flawless, just wait until you see the beautiful photos from her baby bump photoshoot. Having already broken the internet with the news that she is expecting twins with husband of almost ten years, Jay Z, the singer has now shared the rest of her pregnancy pictures with fans – and she has taken things to a whole new level. The collection of images, which appeared without warning on Beyoncé’s official website, show the star submerged underwater, on top of a car and even posing with daughter Blue Ivy, who is sweetly kissing her mum's tummy in one picture….

    Beyoncé and Jay Z’s daughter Blue Ivy, who celebrated her fifth birthday on 7 January, appears in some of the photos. She will become a big sister twice over when her parents welcome their twins later on this year, and is no doubt already very excited to meet the new additions.

    Beyoncé's pregnancy announcement photo shows the star kneeling in front of a giant hedge of roses, wearing blue satin underwear and a maroon bra. She is covered with a long green veil, and is already heavily pregnant. It’s worth noting that Beyoncé has played with veils before; the huge backdrops she danced against during her world tour featured her wearing one, and she similarly appeared in one in the video for in 2014.

    This sweet image shows big sister-to-be Blue Ivy presenting her mum with a flower. The pregnant star shocked fans with the news she was expecting twins when she made the announcement on Instagram on Wednesday - she now hold the Instagram record for the most liked photo.

    Alongside the very first photo of her baby bump, 35-year-old Beyoncé wrote: "We would like to share our love and happiness. We have been blessed two times over. We are incredibly grateful that our family will be growing by two, and we thank you for your well wishes - The Carters."

    Part of the pregnancy photoshoot took place underwater. The singer looks effortlessly stylish and graceful as she poses in billowing yellow robes - her baby bump clearly visible as she floats over pink flowers strewn below her. This photo was one of a number taken in a swimming pool to commemorate Beyoncé's pregnancy.
