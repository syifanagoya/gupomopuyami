Jen Saviano and Boyfriend Landon Ricker Are Engaged! Inside Their Relationship

March 2024 · 19 minute read

Jen Saviano is engaged to her fiance, Landon Ricker!

The pair took to Instagram to announce their engagement and share photos with their fans and followers. Soon, the two were flooded with congratulatory messages and comments.

The pair got engaged after being in a relationship for at least two years. Before the engagement, the couple welcomed a baby boy as well.

Here is everything we know about the pair.

Jennifer Saviano’s relationship with Landon Ricker

It is unknown when Saviano started dating Ricker. Saviano appeared on The Bachelor on Ben Higgins’s season in 2016.

Moreover, she also appeared on Seasons 3 and 6 of Bachelor in Paradise. Therefore, it is safe to assume that Saviano was single till 2019 at least. 

Looking at her Instagram, it seems like Saviano started dating her boyfriend before May 2021. This is because she shared a throwback picture of the duo in May 2022 with the caption, “1 year ago today. hey, @lreezy96 remember when we made a really cute baby shortly after? that was fun!”

The couple went Instagram official with their relationship in January 2022. Saviano introduced her boyfriend to her fans and followers along with the news of her pregnancy.

Ricker, on the other hand, posted two pictures — one of various Polaroids of him and Saviano and another of Saviano smiling widely at the camera while lying on a couch. Ricker captioned the post, “This morning, with her, having coffee.”

Therefore, the pair were together at least from May 2021 or even before that. Saviano got engaged to her boyfriend after celebrating their son’s first birthday.

The pair confirmed their engagement with Saviano uploading engagement images with the message, “Want to do this forever?’ Yes, yes I do.” On the other hand, Ricker wrote, “And we’re just getting started, B. Love you forever.”

Saviano followed up with another post in which she showed off her engagement ring and revealed information about their special day. “some photo deets from engagement day,” she wrote.

She hinted that they were about to get engaged that day. “We toured the property and grounds of our little Villa we were staying at in Umbria, and the last stop was the most serene table setup overlooking the Tuscan hills. Landon asked, “Do you know why I set this up?”… I said, “I think so?” she wrote. 

At the time of writing, the pair has not revealed when they intend on getting married or having their wedding ceremony. 

Jen Saviano, Landon Ricker, and their baby

Saviano announced her pregnancy in January 2022. She showed off her baby bump in an animal-print bikini and showed off her tummy at an ultrasound session in the picture. 

At the same time, Saviano also introduced her boyfriend. They were spotted kissing and caressing one other while cradling her baby bump. 

Since then, Saviano started giving updates about her fiance and baby on Instagram. The pair welcomed their son, Wilder Reed, in April 2023.

She wrote, “He’s here. Wilder Reed. 8lb 10oz of pure heaven. We couldn’t be more in love with him.” She updated her followers on all things new parenthood since Wilder was born and during her whole pregnancy.

She also answered questions regarding her baby’s birth, her labor, and her delivery experience on Instagram stories. She revealed that her pregnancy did not go as planned.

She was six days overdue, and her amniotic fluid was dropping, so they had to schedule an induction. They put her on cytotec (an oral medication), and finally, everything moved smoothly and fast from there.

Fans also inquired about Saviano’s experience with her medical team and whether they adhered to her birth plan throughout her labor. She revealed that the nurses were nice and respected her decision. 

After induction, her contractions picked up, and after pushing for around 45 minutes, she welcomed her healthy baby into the world. A month after giving birth, Saviano went to Instagram to gush about Ricker.

She uploaded a video compilation of him and thanked him for being Reed’s father. She also posted photos of her baby, claiming that she gave birth to an angel.

All in all, the pair are happy to be together with their small family of three.

Late Actor Lance Reddick Had Great Relationship with His Wife and Two Kids

Lance Reddick’s wife, Stephanie Reddick, is deeply saddened by his demise.

Late actor Lance Reddick and his wife, Stephanie Reddick, shared a strong and enduring relationship, built on love and trust, spanning over two decades.

Lance met his wife in 1999 while he was working on a play at the Guthrie Theater in Minneapolis, Minnesota, whereas Stephanie was an employee there. The two quickly became close and developed a romantic connection, leading to a decade-long relationship.

Later, the couple eventually tied the knot in 2011 in a low-key ceremony at Bar Lurcat in Minneapolis, Stephanie’s home state. They also deliberately chose a single venue for both the ceremony and reception to simplify things for out-of-town guests.

During the wedding, the actor set only two rules for the celebration. The first rule was that everyone had to have fun, and the second was that Lance couldn’t see his wife’s wedding dress before she walked down the aisle.

Similarly, Stephanie embraced simplicity in her wedding preparations, allocating a modest $250 for her dress, which the actor personally picked up from the post office. She also chose floral arrangements for the dress details.

Lance Reddick and his wife, Stephanie Reddick, were together for over two decades. (Source: Spamchronicles)

Recalling her relaxed attitude, she shared, “I told the florist I didn’t care about the flower type, as long as they were pretty. She suggested peonies because they were in season. I didn’t even know the flower color until the wedding day, and they turned out beautifully.”

Additionally, she fondly recalled her favorite moment as the day her mom saw her in the dress and moved to tears.

Although the couple openly shared details about their love story and wedding, they intentionally chose to keep the rest of their married life more private, valuing a sense of intimacy and personal space.

However, the actor and his wife made several red-carpet appearances together over the years, attending events such as premieres for movies like White House Down, The Blacklist, and Godzilla vs Kong.

Now, following Lance’s untimely passing due to heart disease, his wife took a moment to connect with her late husband’s fans and express profound gratitude. Using the late actor’s Twitter and Instagram accounts, Stephanie conveyed;

“Lance was taken from us far too soon. Thank you for all your overwhelming love, support, and beautiful stories shared on these platforms over the last day. I see your messages and can’t begin to express how grateful I am to have them.”

In addition to this heartfelt message, she extended special thanks to the thousands of Destiny players who participated in a tribute to Lance, emphasizing, “And to the thousands of Destiny players who played in a special tribute to Lance, thank you. Lance loved you as much as he loved the game.”

Upon seeing the post, an outpouring of condolences and tributes flooded in from the actor’s friends and fans. Halle Berry, a fellow star, shared a poignant comment, “My love and prayers are with the family at this unfortunate time. Lance was one of the most sincere people I’d ever met. Truly one of a kind! RIP sweet king.”

Likewise, Ana Gasteyer shared her condolences, stating, “Sending so much love and sympathy. He was an extraordinary talent and had an amazing career. And he loved his wife and family so much. A life, far too short, but so well lived.”

Lance Reddick has two children, but not with his wife Stephanie Reddick

Lance and his wife, Stephanie, didn’t have children together, although Stephanie likely played a key role in raising Lance’s two kids. The late actor had children with his first wife, Suzanne Yvonne Louis — a daughter named Yvonne Nicole and a son named Christopher Reddick.

As of now, both of his children have grown up. But as Lance deliberately kept them out of the spotlight throughout his life, he didn’t share many pictures of his kids on social media, and neither talked about being a father in any of his interviews.

Inside the marital life of Lance Reddick and his first wife Suzanne Yvonne Louis

Lance married his first wife, jewelry designer Suzanne, in 1986. The details of their initial meeting and the early days of their relationship remain shrouded in mystery.

Shortly after their wedding, the couple decided to part ways. Post-separation, Suzanne became a mother to Kaiya Dane Meier, her child with Karl Meier.

Unfortunately, the actor’s first wife faced a tragic turn of events, suffering a heart attack and falling into a coma from which she never recovered. Later, on November 3, 2011, she passed away, marking a deeply sorrowful chapter in their lives.

After Suzanne’s passing, Lance candidly discussed the profound loss of his first wife and the mother of his two children in an interview with Strombo in 2012.

Reflecting on the impact of the tragedy, the actor emphasized, “It made me realize that I always have to keep in front of me why I’m doing this.”

Lance Reddick with one of his two children (Photo: Facebook)

Sharing the emotional weight of becoming his kids’ sole parent, he stated, “I felt it more with my ex-wife because suddenly I was my kids’ only parent… I have great kids, and it’s because they had a great mom.”

After his first wife’s passing, Lance and his second wife, Stephanie, continued to care for and raise his children, providing them with love and support.

Andy Samberg and his wife Joanna Newsom consistently put effort into their relationship.

Meanwhile, Samberg donned a bespoke suit crafted by the talented David Hart, ensuring that every detail of their attire reflected the elegance and style befitting such a special occasion.

Likewise, the star-studded guest list featured some of Hollywood’s brightest names, such as Amy Poehler, Nick Kroll, Maya Rudolph, Paul Thomas Anderson, Lorne Michaels, and Adam Sandler.

Even though the wedding was filled with laughter, there were moments of genuine emotion as well. According to a source, Samberg’s speech achieved the perfect balance between humor and sentiment, leading to tears from both him and his wife.

Similarly, Newsom later affectionately expressed that her husband is her “favorite person in the world,” praising him as the biggest superfan of her music. She continued, saying, “He’s the person I would most want to hang out with at any given moment.”

Who is Andy Samberg’s wife? Brief on her career

At the young age of four, Newsom started learning the piano, and by the time she was eight, she had already mastered the harp.

Joanna Newsom developed an interest in music since her childhood. (Source: Collapse Board)

She subsequently enrolled at Mills College in Oakland, California, where she delved into the realms of musical composition and creative writing. However, her academic path took a turn when she decided to drop out, as reported by the New York Times.

In a 2004 interview with The A.V. Club, Newsom shared that around eighth grade, she decided that she wanted to be a composer. So, that’s when her focus shifted again, and that’s what she went to college for.

After this transformative period, she evolved into a well-known singer and harpist, celebrated for her literary lyrics and distinctive sound. Newsom first released her EPs in 2002, marking the beginning of her musical career.

Shortly after, she signed with Drag City and made her debut with ‘The Milk-Eyed Mender’ in 2004. This marked the inception of her artistic journey, which further unfolded with the release of her fourth full-length album, Divers, in 2015.

Beyond her musical achievements, Newsom ventured into film and television. Notably, she featured in MGMT’s Kids music video in 2009, played a harpist in a 2012 episode of Portlandia, and took on a role in Paul Thomas Anderson’s Inherent Vice.

Furthermore, she once made a brief cameo in the finale of NBC’s (formerly Fox’s) police drama, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, in 2021. Her husband, Samberg, was one of the lead cast members — he played the role of Detective Jake Peralta.
