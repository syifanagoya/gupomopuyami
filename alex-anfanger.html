    Alex Anfanger Facts

  • He made his first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Lawrence Reitzer in the comedy film, Kids in America, in 2005.
  • He played Noah, the virgin, in the web and television comedy series, Burning Love, in 2013. The show is a spoof of reality dating competition shows like The Bachelor and The Bachelorette.
  • Alex Anfanger appeared as Ted’s Toner Box Associate in the 2013 adventure comedy-drama film, The Secret Life of Walter Mitty, whose lead cast included Ben Stiller, Kristen Wiig, Shirley MacLaine, Adam Scott, Kathryn Hahn, and Sean Penn.
  • He co-created, co-wrote, as well as acted in the comedy television series, Big Time in Hollywood, FL, in 2015.
  • In 2019, Alex Anfanger played Shark Lords in the live-action/adult animated anthology television series, Cake.
  • Alex was cast as Derrick in the 2019 romantic comedy film, Plus One, starring Maya Erskine, Jack Quaid, Beck Bennett, and others.
